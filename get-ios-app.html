How to install

Three steps.
Under a minute.

Open reflecto.co.in/app in Safari on your iPhone, then follow these steps to add it to your Home Screen.

1

Tap the Share button

At the bottom of your Safari browser, tap the Share icon — it looks like a box with an arrow pointing up.

Bottom centre of Safari
2

Tap "Add to Home Screen"

Scroll down in the share sheet until you see Add to Home Screen. Tap it.

It has a "+" icon next to it
3

Tap "Add" to confirm

You'll see a preview with the Reflecto name and icon. Tap Add in the top-right corner. That's it — Reflecto appears on your Home Screen.

Done in under 10 seconds
✦
⚠️

Must be opened in Safari

"Add to Home Screen" only appears in Safari on iOS. If you're in Chrome, Firefox, or another browser, copy the URL and paste it into Safari.

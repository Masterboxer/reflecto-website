Why we exist

Social media was supposed
to bring us closer.

It didn't. We ended up performing for strangers, chasing validation, and feeling emptier after every scroll. Reflecto is a quieter answer.

The old way

Designed to keep you scrolling

Endless feeds, dopamine loops, and follower metrics trained us to perform rather than share. Authenticity became content.

  • Infinite content designed to numb
  • Public audience creates pressure
  • Follower counts replace real connection
  • Algorithms prioritise engagement over truth
The Reflecto way

Designed for the people you love

No audience. No algorithm. Just the handful of people you actually want to stay close to, sharing one honest moment per day.

  • One post — intentional, thoughtful
  • Small circle — safe and intimate
  • No metrics — just real presence
  • Feed ends — you're allowed to stop
One post a day

The limit
is the point.

When you can only post once, you choose what actually matters. Not the funniest moment. Not the most aesthetic photo. Just something true.

It's a constraint that quietly teaches you to pay attention to your own day.
